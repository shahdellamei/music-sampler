Big Sean

Photo by Wikipedia.

Sean Michael Leonard Anderson known as Big Sean was born in 1988. He is an American rapper from Detroit, Michigan. Big Sean signed a contract with Kanye West's GOOD Music in 2007. Followed by Def Jam Recordings in 2008 and then Roc Nation in 2014. After releasing several mixtapes, in 2007, Big Sean released his first official mixtape Finally Famous: The Mixtape which includes twenty tracks. His song "Made" featuring Drake was leaked on the internet in 2010 which was a big hit. Big Sean released his second studio album named Hall of Fame, in 2013. Sean's third and last studio album Dark Sky Paradise was a big hit. It was released in 2015 and made it to the Number one hit on the Billboard 200.
